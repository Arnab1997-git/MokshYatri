# Moksh Yatri Vision

## Mission

Moksh Yatri is an AI-powered travel platform focused on helping travelers discover meaningful journeys beyond traditional tourism.

The platform combines personalized recommendations, hidden gems, community stories, AI-generated itineraries, and intelligent travel planning into a single experience.

---

## Core Philosophy

Journey Beyond Destinations.

Travel is not only about reaching a place. It is about stories, experiences, culture, emotions, people, and self-discovery.

---

## Target Users

### Travelers

Users seeking:
- Personalized travel recommendations
- Unique destinations
- Hidden gems
- Community travel stories
- AI-assisted travel planning

### Travel Consultants

Internal travel planners and consultants who:
- Manage leads
- Create quotations
- Track bookings
- Manage operations
- Deliver personalized travel experiences
